Lily Collins

About Lily Collins

Lily Jane Collins (born 18 March 1989) is an English-American actress and model. She is known for her elegance, versatility, and roles in both drama and comedy. Today, she is one of the most recognizable stars of her generation.

“A star of elegance and talent”

Important Events

  1. 1989 – Born in Guildford, Surrey, England.
  2. 2009 – Breakthrough role in *The Blind Side*.
  3. 2012 – Played Snow White in *Mirror Mirror*.
  4. 2016 – Nominated for Golden Globe (*Rules Don’t Apply*).
  5. 2020 – Worldwide fame with *Emily in Paris*.

Notable Works

  • *Mirror Mirror* (2012)
  • *Love, Rosie* (2014)
  • *To the Bone* (2017)
  • *Emily in Paris* (2020–present)
